## The decisive finding

Physical TCP touches (Flexiv Monitor, World coords) at three board corners showed **both**
hand-eye solutions carried a fixed base-frame error field: ~3.6° rotation + ~27 mm translation —
despite sub-0.5° internal AX=XB consistency, and invariant to rotating the board pattern 90°
(so not a per-view PnP fluke). Sub-region PnP proved the board flat (0.2°); depth at checker
corners was too noisy to referee. Root cause not fully identified (leading hypothesis: a
systematic board-pose orientation bias at this workspace viewing geometry, common to all
sessions); the pragmatic fix below sidesteps it.

## The fix

Rigid (Kabsch) registration of the s003 solution onto the 3 measured touch points:
registration residuals [2.4, 0.9, 2.4] mm; correction = 3.637° / 27.3 mm.
Validation on an independent 4th corner: **3.9 mm** (repo bars: <3 mm good, >5 mm recapture).
